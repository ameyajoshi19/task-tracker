Delete Account & Data

Sortd by The Sortd App Dev

Sortd stores all your data locally on your device. We do not maintain any external servers or cloud databases. Follow the steps below to delete your account and all associated data.

How to delete your data

Step 1: Sign out from the app

Open Sortd → Settings → tap Sign Out. This disconnects your Google account from the app.

Step 2: Uninstall the app

Uninstalling Sortd permanently deletes all locally stored data, including:

  • All tasks (titles, descriptions, durations, deadlines, priorities)
  • Scheduled time blocks
  • Your availability settings
  • Calendar selection preferences
  • All app preferences and cached data
Step 3: Remove Sortd calendar events (optional)

Task events created by Sortd on your Google Calendar will remain after uninstalling. To remove them:

  • Open Google Calendar
  • Find the "Sortd" calendar in your calendar list
  • Delete the calendar to remove all Sortd-created events
Step 4: Revoke app access (optional)

To revoke Sortd's access to your Google account:

  • Go to Google Account Permissions
  • Find Sortd in the list of apps with access
  • Click Remove Access

What data is deleted

  • Immediately on uninstall: All task data, scheduling data, availability settings, preferences, and cached calendar data stored on your device.
  • Manually (Step 3): Calendar events created by Sortd on your Google Calendar.

What data is retained

  • Sortd does not store any data on external servers, so there is no server-side data to retain.
  • Google Calendar events created by Sortd will persist on your Google Calendar until you manually delete them (see Step 3 above).
Note: Since all data is stored locally on your device, data deletion is immediate and permanent upon uninstalling the app. There is no retention period for locally stored data.
