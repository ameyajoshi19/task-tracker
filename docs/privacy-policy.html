Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

Sortd ("we", "our", or "the app") is a task scheduling application for Android. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Google Account Information

  • Your name and email address, used to identify your account within the app.
  • We access this information through Google Sign-In when you set up the app.

Google Calendar Data

  • Free/busy information from calendars you select, used to find available time slots for scheduling tasks.
  • Calendar event data is read to avoid double-booking and detect scheduling conflicts.
  • The app creates events on a dedicated "Sortd" calendar on your Google Calendar to represent scheduled tasks.

Task Data

  • Task titles, descriptions, durations, deadlines, and priority levels that you create within the app.
  • Scheduling preferences such as your weekly availability windows and day preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule your tasks into available time slots on your Google Calendar.
  • To detect scheduling conflicts and propose reschedules.
  • To send you notifications about deadline risks and schedule changes.
  • To sync your task schedule with Google Calendar in the background.

3. Data Storage

  • All task data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database.
  • Calendar events are stored on your Google Calendar via the Google Calendar API.
  • We do not operate any external servers. Your data is never uploaded to or stored on our servers.
  • Scheduling preferences and availability settings are stored locally on your device.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes.
  • The only external service the app communicates with is the Google Calendar API, solely for the purpose of reading your calendar availability and writing scheduled task events.

5. Google API Services

Sortd's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Permissions

  • Internet — Required to communicate with Google Calendar API for syncing.
  • Accounts — Required for Google Sign-In to authenticate your identity.
  • Notifications — Used to alert you about scheduling conflicts and at-risk deadlines.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
  • Calendar events created by Sortd remain on your Google Calendar and can be deleted directly from Google Calendar at any time.
  • You can sign out from the app at any time via Settings, which disconnects your Google account from the app.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Authentication is handled through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol.
  • All communication with Google APIs is encrypted via HTTPS.
  • No sensitive data is stored in plain text.

9. Children's Privacy

Sortd is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
