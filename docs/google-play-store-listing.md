# Google Play Store Listing — Sortd

## App Name
**Sortd — Smart Task Scheduler**

## Short Description (80 chars)
Stop planning, start doing. Sortd auto-schedules tasks on your Google Calendar.

## Full Description

**Your to-do list is full. Your calendar has gaps. Sortd connects the two.**

Ever stare at a long task list wondering *what to work on and when?* Sortd takes your tasks, looks at your actual availability on Google Calendar, and schedules everything into your free time — automatically. No more guessing, no more forgotten deadlines, no more wasted hours.

**How it works:**
1. Tell Sortd when you're available each week
2. Add your tasks with a deadline and estimated duration
3. Tap "Sort it" — Sortd finds the best time slot on your calendar and books it

That's it. Your to-do list becomes a time-blocked schedule in seconds.

---

### Prioritize like a pro
Sortd uses the **Eisenhower Matrix** — the same framework used by CEOs and productivity experts — to organize your tasks into four clear categories:

- **Now** — Urgent and important. Do these first.
- **Next** — Important but not urgent. Plan time for these.
- **Soon** — Urgent but less important. Fit them in.
- **Later** — Neither urgent nor important. Schedule when there's room.

Not sure where a task belongs? Sortd suggests the right priority based on your deadline.

### Smart scheduling that actually works
Most planners just list tasks. Sortd *fills your calendar intelligently*:

- Finds every open slot in your day and matches it with the right task
- A 45-minute gap? Sortd fills it with a task that fits — not one that's too long
- Have a 4-hour project? Mark it as splittable and Sortd breaks it into focused blocks across your week
- Higher-priority tasks always get scheduled first, with deadline-sensitive tasks locked in before they're due

### Your Google Calendar, supercharged
Sortd works *with* your existing schedule, not against it:

- Reads your Google Calendar events so it never double-books you
- Creates a dedicated "Sortd" calendar so your tasks appear right alongside meetings and appointments
- Background sync keeps everything up to date — if a meeting gets added, Sortd adjusts
- Completed tasks are automatically marked on your calendar

### Conflicts? Handled.
Life changes. Meetings get added. Deadlines shift. When your schedule gets disrupted, Sortd doesn't just break — it **proposes a new plan**. Review exactly what moves where, then approve or reject with one tap. You stay in control.

### Never miss a deadline
Sortd watches your deadlines and warns you when a task is at risk of not fitting into your schedule before it's due. No more last-minute surprises.

### Works offline
Create and organize tasks anywhere — on the subway, on a flight, wherever. Sortd saves everything locally and syncs with Google Calendar when you're back online. A gentle reminder lets you know when your schedule data needs refreshing.

### Designed to get out of your way
- **Clean Material Design** with light and dark themes
- **Swipe gestures** to quickly delete or reschedule tasks
- **Pull to refresh** for instant calendar sync
- **Onboarding in 3 steps** — sign in, set your hours, pick your calendars, done
